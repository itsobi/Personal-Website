About Me

From the highest of the highs to the lowest of the lows... I've been through it all. From suffering a season-ending injury my senior year of high school, subsequently losing all of my scholarships, to playing as a walk-on True Freshman at Oklahoma State. Then, transferring the end of my freshman year to a school in West Virginia, which I couldn't even point to on the map (shout out to Marshall U!). To my true senior year at Marshall, having to sit out 9 games my final year due to injury, then Grad-Transferring to The University of Oklahoma amid a global pandemic...

Later on being named a starter for week 1, to receiving a call the Thursday of game week, I would be placed in quarantine for 2 weeks due to a positive COVID-19 test. Only to come back and suffer a season-ending injury, my first practice back. (this is the short version by the way)
