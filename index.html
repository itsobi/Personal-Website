About Me

From the highest of highs to the lowest of lows, I've been through as much adversity as you could think of…

From losing 7 full-ride scholarship offers at the age of 17, due to a fractured/dislocated ankle

To walking on to Oklahoma State and playing as a True-Freshman Walk-on

To then, transferring to a school in a state I had no clue existed and becoming a 3-year starter and captain. (Marshall University, Huntington, WV)

Later, transferring again to The University of Oklahoma for my last year, earning the starting Y receiver spot, then testing positive for COVID-19 the Wednesday of game week, forcing me to miss that game

To then, coming back and suffering a season-ending injury my first practice back…

I say all of this because, no matter what happens to you in life

IT KEEPS GOING

Be able to adapt to whatever this life throws at you

Because the ones that are willing to do so...

Will always come out on top!
